Chicken Biriyani

Description

Who doesnâ€™t love a clean kitchen especially while cooking? This Rice Cooker Chicken Biryani recipe is definitely one to learn for quick and fuss-free cooking!

Delicious, spicy marinated chicken paired with flavourful, fluffy Basmati rice all cooked using just a rice cooker. This classic Indian recipe smells and tastes so good it will leave everybody wanting seconds.

Ingredients

Serving for Two
  • 600 g Basmati rice
  • 300 ml Water
  • 300 ml Chicken Stock
  • 1 tsp Cumin seeds
  • 6 Cloves
  • 10 Cardamom seeds
  • 2 Cinnamon stick
  • 4 tbsp Ghee
  • 1 tsp Salt
Chicken Marinate
  • 1000 g Chicken thigh
  • 2 tbsp Garam masala
  • 2 tbsp Grated ginger
  • 2 tbsp Grated garlic
  • 4 tbsp Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Red chili powder
  • 2 tbsp Turmeric powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cut chicken into chunks, add all ingredients under marinate into a bowl and mix well together with chicken.
  2. Marinade chicken for at least 30 minutes or overnight for softer chicken.
  3. Wash Basmati rice.
  4. Add ghee in a pan over medium low heat, add in cinnamon, cloves, cardamom seeds, cumin seeds and stir fry until fragrant.
  5. Turn up heat and add in marinated chicken. Stir fry until chicken is lightly browned.
  6. Add in washed rice and mix well.
  7. Transfer into a rice cooker, add in chicken stock and water. Make sure rice is submerged.
  8. Set the rice cooker to cook.
  9. Once ready, fluff rice and serve. Garnish with chopped coriander.
